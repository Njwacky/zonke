📖 Zonke Tactical Esports Rules

Welcome to the ultimate **Zonke Street Duel**! Two armies of 12 soldiers face off across the center board.


🔥 SHOOT FIRST OR DIE (`DEAD 💀😭` vs `GOAT 🐐🤣`):

Whenever you unlock all 12 stages on a soldier (`Head -> ... -> Bullet 5`), your soldier is complete and **FIRES FIRST!**
👉 Your opponent's soldier directly across on that same row is immediately **SHOT DEAD (`💀 DEAD 😭`)!**
👉 Once a soldier is dead (`-1`), that player can **never continue using that row**! Landing on a dead row wastes your turn.


🚀 DEDICATED LAUNCH PAD & HORIZONTAL POWER BAR:

To ensure Row 11 and Row 12 (`the bottom rows`) are 100% clean and easy to hit, the paper ball rests in its own **Launch Pad Arena (`🏀 LAUNCH`)** beside the **Horizontal Power Bar** right below the target rows! Flick up and aim freely across all 12 rows without overlap!


⭐ THE CENTER BONUS (`--⭕--`):

Landing on the center dividing badge adds **+1 stage to ALL YOUR ALIVE SOLDIERS at once!** Can complete multiple soldiers and wipe out your opponent in one shot!


🏆 WIN CONDITION:

The first player to complete **5 GOAT Soldiers (`5/9 Majority`)** wins the Tactical Duel!
